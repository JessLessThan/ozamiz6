Ozamiz City

Ozamiz City, located in Misamis Occidental, Philippines, is a peaceful and scenic city known for its rich history and natural attractions. Nestled between Panguil Bay and Mount Malindang, the city offers breathtaking views and a laid-back atmosphere. Ozamiz is home to the historic Fort Santiago (Cotta), a Spanish-era fortress, and the miraculous Birhen sa Cotta Shrine. Visitors can enjoy exploring nearby waterfalls, lush forests, and vibrant local culture, making it a gateway to both adventure and spiritual reflection.
