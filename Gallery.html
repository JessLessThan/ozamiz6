Top 3 Tour Spots in Ozamiz City

1.Editha Farm

Editha Farm is a chemical-free all natural farm. All our farm produce are organically grown and we adhere to the PNS or Philippine National Standards on Organic Agriculture. Editha Farm takes pride of being a pioneer member of Misamis Occidental Organic Agriculture Association (MOOAA).

We, at Editha Farm, believe that the Millennials are the future of farming. This is the reason why we open the farm for everyone who wish to know the basics of Organic Farming. We give lectures and hands on activities to the future farmers and farm owners.

2.Cotta Fort

Cotta Fort is a tourist attraction at Ozamis City, Mindanao wherein the Birhen ng Cotta is enshrine. Hopefully, the local government and or a private sector take action in improving the place.

Cleanliness should be maintain and that the pasalubong Store will be organized. Also, the seaside should be maximized by putting other attractions like restaurants.

3.Aquamarine Park

Rama Beach in Clarin (Misamis Occidental) is a very good for tour spots near in ozamiz city. It is a 10-minute walk from the city center and is a popular spot for sightseeing.

The sea is brown and the sand is black, there are only a few old bungalows to live in, in case of bad weather.
